Chai Recipe

DESCRIPTION

Delicious, authentic chai, passed down from my great-great grandmother. Spicy and sweet and absolutely wonderful!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tsp. sugar
  • 1tsp. loose tea leave

STEPS

  1. Pour all ingredients into a (preferably spouted) saucepan.
  2. Place over medium heat.
  3. Allow to heat until small bubbles appear around the perimeter of the milk.
  4. Stir the chai, scraping the bottom to avoid scalding the milk.
  5. When the milk comes to a boil, turn off the heat and stir well.
  6. Bring to a boil once again, turn off the heat and stir well.
  7. Allow to steep for a few minutes.
  8. Strain carefully into a cup, and serve.
